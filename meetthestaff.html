    Meet The Staff

    We have a team of well trained and experienced teachers. They nurture and polish children with love and dedication, by being sensitive to their individual needs. The teacher-student ratio in DPS Kidszone Bhopal is 1:14 which ensures equal attention to all the kids. Every class has a mother teacher, assistant teacher and a well trained maid.

    Our teachers are dedicated caregivers and are motivated to provide an all-round development of your child. They embrace the view that each child is a unique person with an individual pattern and timing of social, emotional, physical and intellectual development.
