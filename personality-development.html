    • Personality Development

    Children in small groups, listen attentively and talk about their experiences. They pay attention and are able to communicate with other children and adults. Kids develop a rich vocabulary and also learn to speak fluent English. They listen and respond to stories, songs, nursery rhymes and poems. Children enjoy books from our well stocked book corner and handle them carefully. Students of class Prep are given story books on weekend.

    The perfect blend of academics and co-scholastic activities like Dance, Music, Shakespeare Club, PE Class, Swimming, Yoga and Aerobics makes DPS Bhopal Kidszone a unique learning destination.

    With a special emphasis on development of intra personal and communication skills, Circle time activities are planned for children. The buzz sessions are conducted every morning and give an opportunity to the students to buzz

    around. Many competitions, field trips and nature walks are planned across the year for children to foster their development in different domains and also to develop a sense of accomplishment. Educational excursions which help in experiential learning and picnics are organized throughout the session.
