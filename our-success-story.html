    • Our Success Story

    Jagran Social Welfare Society, Bhopal stands proud with more than 25 years of excellence in the field of education.

    With DPS Bhopal’s inception in 2000, JSWS changed the pattern of education from a convent school to a public school within MP. It just not brought about the change in the outlook but has also proved itself in these years with consistent performances in Board Examinations, excellence in sports and cultural activities.

    DPS Bhopal crossed all its milestones successfully and has been awarded as the “Dream School” of MP which in itself speaks of the achievements DPS has been providing in the region.

    With the coming up of DPS Indore in the year of 2003 JSWS expanded its quality of education philosophy from Bhopal to Indore and made students of Indore privileged to get education under the DPS banner.

    It was not too long when DPS Indore was also awarded as one of the “Dream Schools” in the state.

    With its consistent performance and quality of education JSWS, Bhopal has achieved high recognition for its work in the two schools and now to add to its feather and to fulfill the growing popularity of the Schools JSWS, Bhopal has launched a third school Delhi Public School, Kolar Road, Bhopal to reach out to some more students who have been deprived of not being a part of the DPS banner.
