    • E-Learning

    DPS Kidszone Bhopal has had an amazing track record of delivering excellence! We have always provided exemplary education to our students and have ensured their holistic development! With a foundation, our students have been groomed to change the world!

    Then came the pandemic, that changed the game! So we changed the plan!

    We at DPS Kidszone Bhopal, strongly believe that when schools and families work together to support learning, children tend to learn faster and better. With students as our priority, change became the reality and collaboration became our strategy!

    ‘Life without education is not life!’ We were the first school in Bhopal to start online mode of teaching during the pandemic. We ensured smooth transition to Online Education, so our students do not miss on learning.

    Virtual home sessions; ‘the new normal’ are perfect examples of teacher-parent teamwork.

    Not only did E-learning ensure uninterrupted learning, but also enhanced the technical skills of all involved. Ever since, E-learning has been a blessing to one and all!
