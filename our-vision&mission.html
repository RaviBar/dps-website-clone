    • Our Vision & Mission

    Vision:

    "Patience is a teacher’s profile and understanding the child, her tool."

    At KIDSZONE, DPS Bhopal we feel that education is bringing out, what is already there in the pupil’s soul. Education is the child’s passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today. So, the team at Kidszone DPS Bhopal, works reflecting the ethics required to nurture the well aware individuals. As the world is changing every minute, we believe that we need to prepare our children for all the challenges in life. We believe to give these children the confidence to fly on their wings of imagination ; At the same time the roots of responsibility and culture are deep seeded in them.

    We emphasize on:

  • Building self-esteem
  • Building creativity
  • Increasing concentration
  • Developing mental skills
  • Fundamental learning Linguistic intelligence
  • Logical intelligence
  • Visual intelligence
  • Musical intelligence
  • Social intelligence
  • Physical intelligence
  • Reflective intelligence

    • We believe our little toddlers should grow into educated, learned, sensitive global citizens with high self esteem and confidence who would explore the world by their own vision.

    Mission:

    We at DPS Kidszone, Bhopal think of education as the means of developing our greatest abilities becausein each one of us there is a private hope and dream which when fulfilled can be translated into benefitof everyone and become a great strength for our nation. We are focused on our mission of making qualityeducation with all round personality development of our students more accessible and affordable, so thatmany more students can pursue their dream of excellence. It is a mission we believe, will help make theworld a better place. We dedicate ourselves at DPS Kidszone, Bhopal to create human capital for our country and endow the world with beautiful minds. We believe that when our students succeed, countriesprosper and societies benefit.
